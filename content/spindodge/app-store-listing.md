# Spin Dodge — App Store Listing

## Name
Spin Dodge

## Subtitle
Flip. Orbit. Survive.

## Description
Spin Dodge is a one-touch orbital dodge game.

Orbit a neon core. Tap to flip direction. Dodge glowing plasma hazards closing in from every angle.

**Level mode** — clear timed stages with unique modifiers every few levels. Every fifth level is a boss with its own attack pattern: ring waves, homing plasma, direction disruptions, and shrinking orbit.

**Unlimited mode** — no timer. Build combo score with near misses, dodge chains, and multipliers.

Unlock prestige cosmetics by beating bosses and completing weekly challenges. Upgrade your long-term power in the Orbit Tree.
