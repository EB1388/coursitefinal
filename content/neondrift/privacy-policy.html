Neon Drift Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Canonical policy: https://cour.software/neondrift/privacy

Neon Drift is a mobile arcade game by Cour, available on iOS and Android. This page mirrors the live policy for reference and offline copies.

Data stored on your device

Game progress is saved locally (scores, coins, unlocks, achievements, settings, and on Android personal score history). It stays on your device unless you delete the app.

iOS — Game Center

Optional Game Center leaderboards and achievements are handled by Apple under Apple's privacy policy.

iOS — Advertising (Google AdMob)

Optional rewarded ads may use Google AdMob. See Google's privacy policy. App Tracking Transparency may be requested on iOS 14+; you can decline and still play.

Android — Personal scores

The Scores board is personal and local to your device. Scores are not uploaded to Cour servers. Play Games sign-in is not required for the current Android build.

Android — Ads and Play Games

Optional rewarded ads may use Google AdMob (for example, revive after game over). See Google's privacy policy. Watching an ad is optional. Optional Play Games may be added in a future update.

Children

Not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
