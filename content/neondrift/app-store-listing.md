## Description

Neon Drift is a fast arcade survival game with a glowing twist.

Drag anywhere on the screen — your orb follows your finger. Collect green gems, grab cyan power-ups, and dodge red enemies as the arena gets harder every second.

FEATURES
• One-finger drag controls — easy to learn, hard to master
• Combo multipliers for chaining gem pickups
• Daily challenges with bonus coin rewards
• Shop with unlockable neon trails and orb skins
• Achievements and rank titles from Rookie to Drift Legend
• Practice mode to learn without enemies
• Game Center global leaderboard
• Optional rewarded ad to revive after game over

Whether you have thirty seconds or thirty minutes, Neon Drift is built for one more run.
