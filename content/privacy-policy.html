Starhook Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 23, 2026

Starhook is a one-tap arcade game. It does not collect personal data, require an account, show ads, use analytics, or track users.

Data Collection

Starhook stores game preferences and your best score locally on your device using Apple-provided storage. This information is not sent to us or to any third party.

Tracking and Advertising

Starhook does not track you across apps or websites and does not include advertising SDKs.

Children's Privacy

Starhook does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If Starhook's privacy practices change, this policy will be updated before those changes are released.
